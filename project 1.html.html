🎯 UI/UX Showcase

How we solved core problems with design & motion

1. Visual Hierarchy & Clarity

Problem: "Clear product information builds trust."

  • Bold pricing (₹2,999) draws immediate attention.
  • Muted secondary text reduces cognitive load.
  • Generous white space makes products the hero.
Primary (CTAs) Success (In Cart) Text Primary Text Muted

2. Micro-interactions & Feedback

Problem: "Slow navigation" and "Complicated checkout flow".

  • Toast messages confirm every action (Add, Remove, Order).
  • Button state changes (e.g., "Add" → "In Cart" ✅) prevent double-clicks.
  • Hover & 3D tilt make the UI feel responsive and tactile.
👆 Tap to add 💬 Toast feedback

3. 3D Visuals & Animations

Why it matters: Motion guides attention and adds delight.

  • Product cards tilt on hover (perspective + rotateY).
  • Product emoji rotates in 3D on the detail page.
  • Screen transitions use a subtle 3D flip effect.
  • Logo floats with a gentle bounce on login.
🔄 Rotating 3D image 🎨 Card tilt

4. Streamlined User Flow

Problem: "Users prefer fewer checkout steps."

Login Home Browse Detail Cart Checkout Confirm

All actions in one scrollable pane — no endless "Next".

5. Transparency (No Hidden Charges)

Problem: "Hidden charges" and "Clear product information".

  • Full price breakdown (Subtotal + Shipping + Tax) before payment.
  • Discount tags (e.g., -20% OFF) displayed prominently.
  • Original prices struck through for honest comparison.
3D & motion enhance usability and delight
